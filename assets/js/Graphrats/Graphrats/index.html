The New York Times wrote:
“...add a plague of rats to everything else New York faces in trying to rebound from the Covid-19 pandemic”

Based on recent articles from the NYT, this visualization compares rat sighting data from 2020-2021 and asks the question, have rats really taken over since the start of the pandemic?
